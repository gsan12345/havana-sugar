Our Heritage

Centuries of
Cuban Craft

  • 16th Century

    Spanish colonizers plant the first sugarcane fields in Cuba — the island's landscape and economy transform forever.

  • 18th Century

    Cuba becomes the world's largest sugar producer. Rum distilleries spring up alongside the great sugar mills.

  • 19th Century

    The first Havana cigar brands are established. Vuelta Abajo tobacco earns its global reputation for unmatched quality.

  • 20th Century

    Cuban café cubano culture emerges as a cornerstone of daily life. The morning colada becomes sacred ritual.

  • Today

    Havana Sugar honors these four traditions, curating the finest expressions of Cuban craftsmanship for a global audience.
